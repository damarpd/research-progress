### Dynamic discrete choice model and its estimation algorithm with dynamic inconsistent expected utility in an uncertain situation

- A study from [Urata and Hato, (2024)](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.trc.2023.104408).
- Description of DDCM according to Rust (1987) -> a choice mechanism with an evaluation of future utility.
- DDCM introduce expected utility in Markovian choice.
- These models calculate the expected utility using the potential future states and utilities under these states.
