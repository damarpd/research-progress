## Literature review

### Influence of waiting behavior on passenger efficiency during boarding and alighting process on the metro platform

- A study from [Shen et al. (2026)](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.simpat.2026.103260), using Chengdu Metro System as a case study.
- The objective of this study: (1) to build and test a computer simulation model of the boarding and alighting process using real data collected from Chengdu Metro; (2) to study how different levels of crowding inside the train carriage affect how efficiently passengers can board and alight; and (3) to analyze how passengers waiting on the platform affect the time it takes for alighting passengers to walk from the train door to the stairs or escalators.
